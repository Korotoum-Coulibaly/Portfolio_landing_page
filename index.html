Creating this project has been an incredible journey for me. It all started with my passion for web development and my desire to showcase my skills and creativity. Let me take you through the timeline of how this project came to life:

Early Interest in Web Development

March 2022

My fascination with web development began when I stumbled upon an amazing website that blended stunning visuals with seamless functionality. I was captivated by the way websites could convey information, evoke emotions, and create immersive experiences.

Enrolling in Holberton School

September 2022

I knew that if I wanted to pursue my passion for web development and turn it into a career, I needed proper education and guidance. That's when I discovered Holberton School. The school's curriculum and hands-on approach to learning were exactly what I was looking for, and I enrolled without hesitation.

Portfolio Project Requirement

May 2023

During my time at Holberton School, we were given the opportunity to create a portfolio project to showcase our skills to potential employers. I saw this as the perfect chance to combine my passion for web development with my desire to create something meaningful and personal.

Finding Inspiration

June 2023

I started exploring various sources of inspiration, including other websites, design blogs, and art galleries. I wanted my project to reflect my unique personality and style while also demonstrating my technical capabilities. It was important to me that the projectwas not just a requirement but a true reflection of who I am as a developer and designer.
