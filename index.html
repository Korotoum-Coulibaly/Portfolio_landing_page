Business application
for generating and managing quotes.

You can configure the data displayed on the customer interface at any time from your administrator interface. Your team members can view the list of their quotes and associated prospects at any time.

The customer interface allows you to:

  • Save quotes

  • Save Prospect informations

  • Consult previously saved quotationsÂ 

  • Download quotes in PDF format / Print quotes

The administrator interface lets you :

  • Configure the information that will appear on the customer interface

  • View quotations produced by your team members on the customer interface

  • See prospects for whom quotes have been generated at least once

  • And manage both platforms
